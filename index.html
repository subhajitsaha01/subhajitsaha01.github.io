Biography

I am currently a Junior Research Scholar at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India. My broad area of research is Software Engineering. I am currently pursuing my research under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Jibesh Patra.

I have completed my Masters in Computer Applications (MCA) from Sastra Deemed University, Thanjavur, India which was in collaboration with my job at Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. At TCS, I was initially trained as a Graduate Trainee at TCS Ignite Innovation Labs, Chennai, India and then I was selected as an Assistant Faculty Trainer for the TCS Initial Learning Program (ILP), Kolkata, India. After spending about an year as an assistant trainer, I transitioned to the role of Senior Faculty Trainer and was co-incharge of the TCS ILP Kolkata Technical Team.

During this period, I was also associated with Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India from where I pursued two Diplomas (Diploma in Programming and Diploma in Data Science).

I have also completed my Bachelors in Sciences(B.Sc. Honors) in Computer Science from St. Xavier's College (Autonomous), Kolkata (Under University of Calcutta). For my Bachelor's dissertation, I was guided by Prof. Debabrata Datta.
